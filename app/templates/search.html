Search Tips
  • 🔍 Flexible search - Use keywords that would realistically appear within a short 8–12 second clip.
  • 🔒 Use "quotes on your search term" for strict search (exact match), this is sensitive and may return fewer results, not recommended.
  • ✂️ Keep it concise — long or complex sentences often won't match well.
  • 🧠 Remember: transcriptions do contain errors (e.g. typos or misheard words).
  • ❌ Avoid searching for single common words like "the", "hello", or "thanks" — they usually appear too frequently or are ignored.
    However, distinctive single words like "curry" or "passport" works well.
  • ⏱️ Note on performance: If a search takes too long (usually 10+ seconds), it may time out early and and just show you the partial results.
